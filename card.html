Chip n Dale

Chip n Dale are often mistaken as brothers, but they are actually best friends.

  • Debut: 1943's Private Pluto
  • Home: Duckburg
  • Occupation: Rescue Rangers
  • Goal: To solve crime and help others

Chip n Dale are energetic chipmunks. They love to eat and make new friends. Chip is the brains of the duo while Dale is typically the follower. Chip can be identified by his small chocolate chip nose and his two teeth that are together while Dale has a large meatball nose and two teeth far apart.
